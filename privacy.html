Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Atlas collects minimal information to provide you with the best gaming experience:

  • Game Performance Data: Your scores, completion times, and game progress
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, and screen resolution for optimal performance
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with our games to improve user experience

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display your scores on leaderboards
  • Track your progress across different game modes
  • Improve game performance and user experience
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Local storage on your device for game progress
  • Secure cloud storage for leaderboard data
  • Encrypted transmission of all data
  • Regular security audits and updates

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics, Yandex Metrika, and Umami for usage insights
  • Hosting: Vercel for reliable website hosting
  • Maps: OpenStreetMap for geographical data

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Atlas is designed to be family-friendly and educational. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us through our main website.

Note: This privacy policy may be updated periodically. We will notify users of any significant changes through our website.
